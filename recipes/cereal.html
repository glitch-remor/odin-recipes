CEREAL

Description

This recipe is passed down from generations in my family. There is only one correct way to make this dish.

Ingredients

  • Cereal of your choice, I prefer shredded mini wheats.
  • Milk of your choice, I prefer skim.
Steps
  1. Grab a bowl.
  2. Pour 1 cup of cereal into your bowl.
  3. Pour 2/3 cup of milk into the bowl.
