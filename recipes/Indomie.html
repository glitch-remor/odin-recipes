INDOMIE

Description

This is one of my favorite fast dishes to eat! I tried them when my sister brought some back to the states.

Ingredients

  • green onions, 1/4 cup
  • Indomie, 1 package
  • water, 2/3 cup
Steps
  1. Grab a medium sized pan.
  2. Bring water to a boil.
  3. Add in indomie noodles, set packets to the side
  4. While indomie is cooking, mix contents of packets into a bowl.
  5. Once indomie is cooked to your liking, I enjoy mine al dente, strain.
  6. Add indomie into bowl you mixed packets in.
  7. Stir until noodles are coated.
  8. Top with green onions.
